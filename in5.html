DREAMS FROM NEPAL

By Bikul Koirala

Review:

An inspiring tale of ambition, resilience, and the transformative power of education set against the rich cultural backdrop of Nepal. Koirala's debut novel introduces readers to Ram, a twelve-year-old boy from a humble village in Nepal, whose aspirations reach far beyond the traditional life laid out before him. An unexpected opportunity propels Ram into a world of possibilities, challenging him to navigate the complexities of personal growth, cultural expectations, and the pursuit of one's dreams. Through evocative storytelling, Koirala captures the essence of Nepali rural life, juxtaposed with the universal themes of hope and determination. Ram's journey is both heartwarming and thought-provoking, offering readers insight into the struggles and triumphs of a young boy striving to define his own destiny. Dreams from Nepal serves as a testament to the enduring spirit of those who dare to dream beyond their circumstances, highlighting the profound impact of faith, love, and unwavering determination.
